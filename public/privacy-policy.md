# Privacy Policy
Throughline — Last updated: March 2, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Throughline ("we," "our," or "us"). Your thoughts are yours — protecting them is our top priority. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## 2. Information We Collect
- Account info: name and email
- Journal entries, mood ratings, to-dos, habits, goals
- Basic usage and device info for troubleshooting
We do NOT collect location, contacts, or camera data.

## 3. How We Use It
- To run and improve the app
- To generate AI reflections (processed in real time, not stored)
- To send account emails like password resets
We do NOT sell your data or use it for advertising.

## 4. Data Storage
Stored securely via Supabase, encrypted at rest and in transit. Your entries are private and never shared with other users.

## 5. AI Processing
We use Claude by Anthropic to generate insights. Your entries are sent to Anthropic's API but not stored or used to train models. See anthropic.com for their policy.

## 6. Data Sharing
Only with: Supabase (database), Anthropic (AI), Vercel (hosting) — all contractually bound to protect your data. Or if required by law.

## 7. Your Rights
- Access your data anytime
- Delete your account and all data within 30 days — email us
- Correct account info in settings
Contact: privacy@throughline.app

## 8. Children's Privacy
Not intended for users under 13. We will delete any data from users under 13 if discovered.

## 9. Changes
We'll notify you of significant changes by email or in-app.
