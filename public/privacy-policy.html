Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Throughline ("we," "our," or "us"). Your thoughts are yours — protecting them is our top priority. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account info: name and email
  • Journal entries, mood ratings, to-dos, habits, and goals
  • Audio recordings when you use the voice-to-text feature (transcribed in real time, not stored after transcription)
  • Purchase history for subscriptions, processed via RevenueCat and Apple
  • Basic usage and device info for troubleshooting

We do not collect location, contacts, or camera data.

3. How We Use It

  • To run and improve the app
  • To generate AI reflections and plans (processed in real time)
  • To manage your subscription and account
  • To send account emails like password resets

We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

4. Data Storage

Stored securely via Supabase, encrypted at rest and in transit. Your entries are private and never shared with other users.

5. AI Processing

We use Claude by Anthropic to generate insights. Your entries are sent to Anthropic's API but are not stored on Anthropic's servers and are not used to train models. See Anthropic's privacy policy for details.

6. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

  • Supabase (database hosting)
  • Anthropic (AI processing)
  • RevenueCat and Apple (subscription management)
  • Vercel (web hosting)

All are contractually bound to protect your data. We may also disclose data if required by law.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your data anytime in the app
  • Delete your account and all data within 30 days — email us
  • Correct your account info in Settings

8. Children's Privacy

Throughline is not intended for users under 13. We will delete any data from users under 13 if discovered.

9. Changes

We'll notify you of significant changes by email or in-app.

10. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@gethroughline.com.
